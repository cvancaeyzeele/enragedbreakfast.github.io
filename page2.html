Arsenal Football Club

Est. 1886

The History

Arsenal Football Club was founded in 1886 by a group of workers from the Woolwich armaments in South London. The club was originally known as Dial Square, but was also named Royal Arsenal and Woolwich Arsenal before becoming Arsenal Football Club.

Arsenal has won the Premier League (as it is now known) 13 times, 12 FA Cups, 2 League Cups, and 14 FA Charity/Community Shields. The club currently is 1st in the Premier League, and holds the record for the longest unbeaten run in England.
